Reviews

The Rose Quartz Facial Serum is a luxurious treatment that delivers visible results from the first use. Formulated with natural ingredients, this serum helps to:

  • Deeply hydrate and plump the skin
  • Reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles
  • Brighten dull complexion
  • Improve skin elasticity and firmness
  • Protect against environmental stressors

Apply 2-3 drops to clean skin morning and night, gently pressing into face and neck. Follow with your favorite moisturizer.

Aqua (Water), Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice, Glycerin, Sodium Hyaluronate, Rosa Damascena Flower Water, Rosa Canina Fruit Extract, Tocopheryl Acetate (Vitamin E), Panthenol, Allantoin, Xanthan Gum, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin.

100% Natural Origin | Cruelty-Free | Vegan | Paraben-Free | Sulfate-Free

Our Story

Ziba Cosmetics was born from a passion for clean, effective beauty products that celebrate your natural radiance. Founded in 2018 by beauty enthusiast Layla Amin, our mission is to provide high-performance cosmetics and skincare that are as good for your skin as they are beautiful.

The Beginning

After years of struggling to find clean, effective beauty products that actually worked, Layla decided to create her own. What started as a small line of handcrafted serums has grown into a full collection of skincare and cosmetics loved by beauty enthusiasts worldwide.

"I wanted to create products that I would feel good about using every day - effective, luxurious, and made with ingredients I could trust." - Layla Amin, Founder

Our Philosophy

At Ziba, we believe beauty should be simple, joyful, and inclusive. Our products are designed to enhance your natural features while nourishing your skin with clean, plant-based ingredients.

  • 100% Cruelty-Free
  • Clean, Natural Ingredients
  • Sustainable Packaging
  • Inclusive Shade Ranges
  • Transparent Labeling

Our Promise

We're committed to creating products that are good for you and good for the planet. Every Ziba product is formulated without parabens, sulfates, phthalates, synthetic fragrances, or other harmful ingredients. We're constantly working to reduce our environmental impact through sustainable packaging and responsible sourcing.
